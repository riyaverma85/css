Explore AI-Powered Tools for Image Editing

Go beyond a simple AI image generator with PicLumen's AI tools—fine-tune every detail of your creations.

Background Remover

Remove background in a click effortlessly.

Image Upscaler

Our AI upscaling technology can deliver crisp and clear images, significantly enhancing the detail and quality of your photos.

It upgrades the image resolution while enlarging the picture to ensure a clear and vivid visual.

PicLumen AI image generator offers several options for enlargement and the flexibility to manually adjust the redraw strength to precisely control and correct image imperfections.

Image Colorizer

Bring black-and-white images & photos to life with vibrant, natural colors. Simply select the image, describe your color ideas, and click submit, our cutting-edge AI image generator will give you a stunning result.

Image Reference

Our image-to-image AI offers three types of image references: character reference to preserve facial consistency; content reference to reuse the outline of the reference image to create a new one; and style reference to assimilate and apply the aesthetic of a given image to your creations.
